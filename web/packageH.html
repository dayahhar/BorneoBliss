Package H Details - 4D3N LUBOK KASAI JUNGLE EXPERIENCE

Includes guided tour, accommodation, meals

Tour Highlights

  • Immerse yourself in the local culture at Lachau Bazaar, known for its vibrant shops and small native market.
  • Embark on a picturesque drive to Batang Ai and take in the scenic beauty along the way.
  • Interact with the Iban community and learn about their culture as you visit the Nanga Sumpa longhouse.
  • Swim in the pristine waters of the stunning Enseluai Waterfall and enjoy a riverside BBQ lunch.
  • Explore the lush rainforest with guided treks, keeping an eye out for exotic wildlife.

Overview of the Lubok Kasai Jungle

Embark on a captivating 4-day adventure in Borneo's rainforest. Start with exploring Lachau Bazaar, then drive to Batang Ai reservoir. Board a longboat to Nanga Sumpa, visit the longhouse, and interact with the Iban community. Hike to Enseluai Waterfall and trek through orangutan habitat. Enjoy daily wildlife encounters and cultural immersion, concluding with a return to Kuching.
