Package B Details - 2D1N MOUNT KINABALU CLIMB

Tour Highlights

  • See the majestic Mount Kinabalu
  • Climb Mount Kinabalu via Timpohon Trail
  • Private room stay with ensuite bathroom at Rata Lebom Resthouse
  • Reach Low's Peak, Borneo's highest summit at 4,095.2m asl

Things You Should Prepare for Mountain Climb

  • Waterproof backpack to store your items
  • Trekking or running shoes with good grip
  • Warm clothing / long sleeves shirt / hiking pants
  • Jacket / wind breaker
  • Extra clothing and socks
  • Small towel
  • Hand gloves
  • Disposable raincoats
  • Head torch (compulsory)
  • Refillable water bottle
  • Personal toiletries
